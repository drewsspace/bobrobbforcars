About Bob

A career built on handshakes.

Bob Robb has spent more than forty years on the automotive side of the table — first as a salesman, then a leasing specialist, now as the person his clients send their families, their neighbors, and their companies to.

His home base is Suburban Cadillac Buick at the Troy Motor Mall, the flagship of the Suburban Collection. That tenure is the quiet foundation of the broker practice: four decades of trust, a rolodex of sales managers at dealerships from Michigan to the coasts, and the professional credibility to move a transaction through any brand, in any state, quickly and without drama.

What clients hire Bob for isn’t access — the internet has democratized that. They hire him for judgment. Which model is actually worth the premium. Which trim will hold value. Which dealership will honor the deal after the ink dries. Those answers only come from forty years of watching it play out.
